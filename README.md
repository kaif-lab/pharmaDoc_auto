# DocuPharma AI

> AI-powered pharmaceutical regulatory document automation platform  
> Built with **React 18** · **Vite** · **Zustand** · **Node.js** · **Express** · **Anthropic Claude**

---

## What is DocuPharma AI?

DocuPharma AI is a full-stack micro-SaaS that automates the creation, review, and localisation of pharmaceutical regulatory documents. It exposes **six fully functional AI modules** — each powered by Claude Sonnet — covering the entire regulatory writing lifecycle from first-in-human IND through to NDA/MAA submission.

**Regulatory standards supported:** ICH E3, E6(R2), E8(R1), E9(R1) · FDA 21 CFR Part 11 & Part 314 · EMA Directive 2001/83/EC · PMDA · MHRA · CDSCO
